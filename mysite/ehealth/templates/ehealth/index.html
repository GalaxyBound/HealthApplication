Reater

Reater stands for Real Time Emergency Responder. This revolutionary application allows patinets with Myocardial Infarction. It measures the following different vitals of the patinets to give overall and specific data on the patinet in real time.

ECG: This is used to get the patients Heart Rate.

Temperature Sensor: This is used to get the patinets body temperature

ECG: This is used to monitor the patients Blood Oxygen Saturation Levels

The system is used by both Patients and Resopnders. Responders are registered and are notified on anyoen in the local area that is in need of assistance and are also give a map of where they are located and where the nearest ADE is. The system has built in functions for the patients so that if they collaspe the system automatically calls the ambulance.
